Crate Flow Frenzy

Match colored racks, slide them across the grid, and clear the loading zone in a calm puzzle built around size, color, and careful planning. When a rack lines up with the right plate, it snaps forward with a smooth truck-loading animation—satisfying motion for a laid-back brain teaser.

Grid & sizing

Each rack has a footprint—park it across the correct number of cells and align it with matching plates before you roll toward the dock.

Paths & obstacles

Other racks and blockers get in the way. If a move cannot finish, the piece returns to where it started, so timing and route choice matter.

Helpful boosts

Extra time, added moves, hints, and level skips can keep a run going when the clock is tight—pick them up as you play.

Why it feels good: simple rules, tactile slides, and polished loading moments that reward the right match—puzzle focus without harsh pressure.
